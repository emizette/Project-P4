Our History

Sweet Breeze Bakery was founded in 2025 by Emilette, a passionate baker who dreamed of bringing authentic, handcrafted baked goods to the community. What started as a small corner shop with just three ovens has blossomed into a beloved neighborhood institution.

Over the past years decades, we've maintained our commitment to traditional baking methods while embracing innovation. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, each one refined and perfected with love and care. From our signature butter croissants to our decadent chocolate cakes, every item tells the story of our journey and dedication to excellence.

Today, Sweet Breeze serves thousands of happy customers each week, but we've never forgotten our roots. We're still the same family-run bakery that believes in quality over quantity, craftsmanship over convenience, and community over commerce.

Our Values

Quality First: We use only the finest ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers. No shortcuts, no compromises. Every pastry, cake, and bread that leaves our kitchen meets our exacting standards.

Community Connection: We believe in supporting our local community. From sourcing ingredients from nearby farms to hosting baking classes for children, we're committed to giving back to the neighborhood that has supported us for so long.

Sustainability: We're mindful of our environmental impact. We minimize waste, use eco-friendly packaging, and work with suppliers who share our commitment to sustainable practices.

Authentic Craftsmanship: In an age of mass production, we remain dedicated to artisanal methods. Our bakers arrive before dawn to ensure everything is made fresh daily, by hand, with skill and passion.
